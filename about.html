What we do

  • C Wel Optometry is an independent optometry practice situated Glen Waverley.
  • Situated 500m from the Glen, the practice is in a convenient location, yet far enough away from the bustle of conventional shops.
  • We offer a more relaxed environment to provide thorough optometric and optical services as patient care is our primary goal.

  • We provide comprehensive eye examinations
  • We offer a wide range of different lenses and frame brands
  • We provide the detection of eye diseases such as glaucoma and age-related Macular Degeneration.
  • As therapeutically qualified optometrists, we can also manage conditions such as eye infections and inflammation.
  • We focus on managing myopia in children with the best methods available, whether it can be with Orthokeratology (OK lens) or low dose atropine.
  • In addition, we also offer general and specialised Rigid Gas Permeable contact lens for conditions such as Keratoconus.
